05 — Recommendations

Proposed Actions

1

Increase Rear Anti-Squat via Suspension Geometry

A +15% anti-squat adjustment raises the rear load baseline from 1079 N to 1241 N static, eliminating spin entirely while preserving full throttle authority. This is the only strategy that improves the physical situation rather than managing it electronically. Achievable via swingarm pivot height or linkage ratio adjustment.

Hardware · Geometry
2

Traction Control Calibration

Implementing TC with an 80% load-ratio threshold eliminates spin with zero additional hardware. Reduces peak throttle by ~44% during the critical 0.4–0.8 s window. Best used in combination with strategy 1 as a safety net.

Software · Immediate
3

Lower Centre of Mass

A 100 mm CoM reduction reduces the Mozzi axis pitch moment arm, decreasing peak |h| by 13%. Achievable through rider position or suspension lowering. Moderate effect on its own; valuable combined with anti-squat.

Setup · Medium Term
4

Rider Technique: Delay Peak Throttle

Delaying full throttle application by ~0.3 s until lean angle drops below 15° ensures the Mozzi axis pitch has returned toward zero before maximum traction demand. Minimal measurable effect on lap time (<0.1 s), eliminates the spin risk.
